Focus: Cybersecurity

Fifteen years in technology, the last five spent entirely focused on cybersecurity — specifically the parts that involve people. I've found that the most effective security improvements don't start with tools. They start with building the right workflows across the right teams and making sure the people inside an organization actually understand why security decisions get made.

All of that work has happened in healthcare, where the consequences of getting it wrong extend beyond data. I've built incident response programs, run tabletop exercises, and sat in the captain's chair when real incidents unfolded. That experience shapes how I think and what I write about.

This blog covers security awareness, incident response program design, and the gap between Security Theater and the things that actually move the needle.
